# Finance Performance Dashboard  
### Sales, Profit & Discount Analysis

## Project Overview

This project presents a **Finance Performance Dashboard** built using Power BI to analyze sales, profit, discounts, and business segments.

The objective is to provide **financial insights** that help stakeholders understand:
- Revenue and profitability trends
- Segment-wise and country-wise performance
- Discount impact on profit
- Seasonal patterns in sales and profit

## Key Business Questions Explored

- How do sales and profit trend over time?
- Which segments contribute most to profit?
- How do discounts impact profitability?
- Which countries and products drive higher profit?
- Are there seasonal patterns in sales and profit?

---

## Key Insights (High-Level)

- Certain business segments consistently generate higher profit
- High sales do not always translate into high profit
- Discounts can negatively impact profit in specific periods
- Clear seasonality exists in sales and profit trends

*(Insights are descriptive and intended for financial performance understanding.)*
